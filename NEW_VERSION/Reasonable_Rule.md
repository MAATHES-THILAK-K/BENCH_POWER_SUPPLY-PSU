## 🔥 3D Print Pricing Rules

### 1. Base Print Cost
Print Cost = (9 × m) + (0.75 × n)  
- m = hours  
- n = grams  

---

### 2. Profit Multiplier (apply on print cost)
- 0.12 mm → ×2.10  
- 0.16 mm → ×1.90  
- 0.20 mm → ×1.70  
- 0.28 mm → ×1.50  

After Profit = Print Cost × Multiplier  

---

### 3. Failure Buffer  
After Failure = After Profit × 1.15  

---

### 4. Human Charge  
+ ₹15  

---

### 5. Double Color Rule  
Apply before profit:  
New Print Cost = Print Cost × 2.2  

---

## 🧮 Final Formula

**Single Color:**  
Final = [(9m + 0.75n) × Multiplier × 1.15] + 15  

**Double Color:**  
Final = [(9m + 0.75n) × 2.2 × Multiplier × 1.15] + 15  

---

## 🚀 Business Rules
- Always round up (₹31 → ₹35, ₹68 → ₹70)  
- Minimum charge: ₹25–₹30 per job
